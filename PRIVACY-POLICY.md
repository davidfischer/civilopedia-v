Civilopedia V Privacy Policy
============================

Last updated: November 30, 2023

This app does not collect any personal data whatsoever. It is not possible to create an account or share any personal data with us. The app is self contained and does not connect to any central service or even use the internet.

While it is unlikely, we may update Our Privacy Policy. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
